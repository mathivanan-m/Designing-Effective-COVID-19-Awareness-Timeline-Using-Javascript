Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) awareness
WASH YOUR HANDS

Wash your hands with soap and warm water regularly for atleast 20 seconds.
COVER A COUGH OR SNEEZE

Cover your cough or sneeze with your tissue. Dispose of tissue and wash your hand afterwords.
FACE MASK

Always cover your nose and mouth with face mask . Don't touch your face often.
SOCIAL DISTANCING

Maintain social distancing in public places , super markets , banks etc; . Stay 6 feet away from other people.
STAY HOME

If you experience respiratory systems like cough or fever , stay home.
PRACTICE RESPIRATORY HYGIENE

Make sure you, and the people around you, follow good respiratory hygiene. This means covering your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze.
CLEAN AND DISINFECT

Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces.
GET HELP

If you experience symptoms of COVID- 19 ( cough or fever , shortness of breath) , call your health care provider.
KEEP UP TO DATE ON THE LATEST INFORMATION FROM YOUR LOCAL AND NATIONAL HEALTH AUTHORITIES

Local and national authorities and public health units are best placed to advise on what people in your area should be doing to protect themselves.
KNOW THE FULL RANGE OF SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are fever, dry cough, and tiredness. Other symptoms that are less common and may affect some patients include loss of taste or smell, aches and pains, headache, sore throat, nasal congestion, red eyes, diarrhoea, or a skin rash.
